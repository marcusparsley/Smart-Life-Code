What is Smart Lighting?

Smart lighting is lighting that you can control from an app, this can be via voice control or smartphone/ tablet use. Smart bulbs (wifi bulbs) are amongst the most common way of getting smart communication relatively hassle free.

Smart lighting makes a great difference in your home with the ability to set timers, sequences and control lighting colour with the majority of the products now available.

Bulbs

Smart bulbs are the perfect choice for anyone looking to improve their home, they offer a number of advantages to normal bulbs straight out of the gate. Here are a few...
-They are dimmable which without the correct switch and bulb would not have been possible beforehand
-They can be controlled from anywhere in the home which makes leaving the kitchen light on when you go to bed a thing of the past. In some cases these bulbs can be controlled from anywhere in the world)
-The majority of the bulbs you buy can now colour change to set any ambient mood you would like

Switches

Smart Switches are an alternative to smart bulbs, or an asset to them for an abundance of control. With the switches you get 100% power from the main so there is no worries about needing to change the batteries. These switches also come with manual controls so in the event of your wifi being down or someone not knowing, you can still control the lights.
