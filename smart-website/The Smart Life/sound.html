Home Speakers

Smart speakers are speakers that not only play music from a device via bluetooth or aux, but can also use voice assistance to search for songs, podcasts and much much more. The most common voice-controlled personal assistants are controlled using Amazon’s Alexa and Google’s Google Assistant. Apple’s Siri is, of course, the brains behind the HomePod speaker. These speakers can also be the hub for controlling many other smart devices such as lighting devices, heating devices and much more.
